Why Homeowners Choose BWF as Their Cabinet Makers

Four decades of cabinetmaking, in one workshop, under one family name.

01

Built, Not Assembled

Every custom cabinet is fabricated from raw lumber in our own shop — no catalog orders, no prefab. Every joint, door, and drawer built to your kitchen's exact dimensions by our own cabinet makers.

02

Finishes That Endure

Every kitchen cabinet leaves our shop with hand-applied finishes built up in layers — the same techniques we use restoring century-old furniture. Built to resist Florida humidity for decades.

03

One Point of Contact

The people who design your kitchen are the people who build and install it. No subcontracted crews, no handoffs, no surprises on installation day.

04

Bilingual Service

We work fluently in English and Portuguese, serving homeowners, designers, and builders across the greater Orlando region.
