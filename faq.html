Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom millwork, cabinetry, restoration, and upholstery services across Central Florida.

What areas does Brandt Wood Finishing serve?

Brandt Wood Finishing serves all of Florida, with primary focus on Central Florida — including Winter Garden, Orlando, Ocoee, Windermere, Clermont, Kissimmee, and the greater Orlando metro area. We also serve the Orlando-to-Miami corridor for larger residential millwork projects.

What services does Brandt Wood Finishing offer?

We specialize in custom millwork and fine woodworking, including custom cabinetry and built-ins, library walls, custom staircases, wall paneling, mantels and fireplace surrounds, antique furniture restoration, and fine residential and commercial upholstery. Every project is handcrafted to specification — no production lines, no shortcuts.

How long has Brandt Wood Finishing been in business?

Brandt Wood Finishing was founded in 1985. With over 40 years of experience in residential custom millwork and woodworking, we are one of Central Florida's most established fine woodworking companies. We are family-owned and operated, headquartered in Winter Garden, FL.

Does Brandt Wood Finishing do commercial millwork?

Yes. In addition to our residential work, we also take on commercial millwork projects, including medical offices, corporate environments, and retail spaces throughout Central Florida. We work directly with general contractors and building owners.

How do I request a quote?

You can reach us by phone at (689) 250-1209 (English — Asaph Brandt) or (754) 215-0045 (Português — Lucas Brandt), fill out the contact form on our website at brandtwf.com, or send a message via WhatsApp. We serve clients throughout Florida and typically respond within 24 hours.

Does Brandt Wood Finishing restore antique furniture?

Yes. Antique furniture restoration is one of our core specialties. We handle structural repair, refinishing, stripping, staining, and color matching for heirloom and antique pieces. Our craftsmen have decades of experience restoring fine furniture to its original condition.
