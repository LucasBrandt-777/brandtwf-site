General

Custom millwork refers to architectural woodwork crafted to exact specifications. Brandt Wood Finishing specializes in wall paneling, custom staircases, library walls, mantels and fireplaces for luxury homes across Florida.

Millwork refers to architectural woodwork built into the structure of a space — wall paneling, wainscoting, crown molding, staircases, mantels, and library walls. Cabinetry is freestanding or semi-attached storage. BWF specializes in both, and many projects combine custom cabinetry within a larger millwork design.

Brandt Wood Finishing was founded in 1985 and has over 40 years of experience crafting premium custom millwork and woodwork for Florida homeowners, designers, and contractors.

All fabrication and installation is performed by BWF's own team — never subcontracted. This ensures consistent quality control from the first cut to the final coat, and full accountability on every project.

BWF focuses on custom projects where craftsmanship is the priority. We do not have a strict minimum, but our work is best suited to projects with a scope that justifies custom fabrication — typically starting around $2,500 for standalone pieces and scaling to full-room or whole-home millwork packages.
