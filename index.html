Why Choose BWF

Florida’s Premier Custom Millwork Craftsmen

At Brandt Wood Finishing, custom millwork and fine woodwork are more than a product — they are a statement of who you are. Based in Winter Garden and serving all of Florida, every piece we create is designed to elevate your space and endure for generations.

Founded in 1985, BWF has spent four decades mastering every facet of architectural woodwork — from hand-crafted library walls and fireplace mantels to floor-to-ceiling kitchen cabinetry and custom staircases. We work directly with homeowners, interior designers, architects, and general contractors across Central Florida and the entire I-4 and US-1 corridors from Jacksonville to Miami. Our shop in Winter Garden combines traditional joinery techniques with precision modern tooling, ensuring every joint, profile, and finish meets the standards of fine furniture, not standard construction.

What separates BWF from production millwork shops is the absence of templates. Every project is drawn from your specifications, built in our facility, and installed by our own team — never subcontracted. Species selection, hardware sourcing, finishing schedules, and site coordination are handled in-house. This is how a family business founded on craftsmanship has maintained a flawless client record for over 40 years.
